WHAT TO EXPECT

Professionalism is best for success!

Every client we have is unique, and we enjoy getting to know you. That process has a lot of steps, and at Driftless Media we offer a transparent service to guide you through it. Below are the main steps in almost any media project, so it helps to have an idea of what you can expect.

PRE-PRODUCTION

All scripting, planning, staging, and scheduling is done here. This process is important, it sets the tone for our best possible outcome. Our team will work alongside with your designated team member step-by-step to ensure that we are on path for success. Together, we are gaining ground now.

Production

Lights. Camera. Action. Production is the creative process in progress and it can be visualized. We must say, it is very exciting.

Post-Production

We are polishing the masterpiece. It’s the final touch. The time frames for Post-Production vary between services, and some go on indefinitely as a subscription base. All projects are different, so that’s why we take the time to explain exactly what needs to happen for the best results. Congratulations, we have achieved your goals.

The Consultation

Hooray! We are very excited to meet you! To help you tell Your Story, we need to discover your goals. We usually can determine this in about one or two conversations, and depending on your gift of gab, we usually spend a few hours on this. This part is really fun, and free!

The Proposal

Great, we have asked the right questions, now we want to send you our ideas of how we can help. We try to keep the proposal brief, it’s is to make sure we are ready to write up our contract with you. Once it is signed, we move on! Another great part of the process that is super fun, and free!

The Contract

The Contract is the specifics. The project plans and expectations are included, and at this time once signed, we collect a deposit. Hold on to your seats, because it begins to take off from here!

Do you Love us Yet

We believe in good communication. It is a requirement for quality media, and that is why we are here to go through your project step by step with you. To achieve your goals, we will commit to a professional process that will allow for effective, efficient, and productive results.
